Sunsource

More sustainable, efficient, and cost-effective energy solution for your home.

Sunsource is the easiest way to find and install solar products for your home. With a wide range of solar panels, you can connect with reputable installer and get expert guidance from Sunsource's Energy Advisors. sunsource makes it easy to find the right products to meet your energy needs.

Problem

Improve the efficiency of solar installation processes

Installing solar panels for homes and businesses requires a lot of coordination between installers and administrators. As a result, installers and administrators are faced with multiple challenges, including inefficient scheduling, miscommunication, and delays in completing projects.

Therefore, the need for a web application that enables installers and administrators to effectively manage solar panel installation projects is crucial to increase productivity, reduce errors, and enhance the overall customer experience.

Goal

A seamless platform for solar installers and administrators to manage solar projects

The goal is to provide a seamless and efficient platform for managing solar installation projects. We should streamline communication between installers and admin, provide real-time updates on project status, and enable efficient project management from start to finish. The goal is to improve the overall efficiency and profitability of solar installation projects through effective project management.

UX Research

Findings

Through user interviews and surveys, we found that installers and admin often struggle with managing project timelines, communicating with customers, and tracking project status. They also expressed a need for a centralized platform that integrates with other tools and simplifies project management.

Pain Points

Users reported frustration with the lack of transparency and communication between team members, as well as difficulty tracking project progress and accessing important project information. Many also expressed a need for better tools for managing customer relationships and streamlining project workflows.

Wireframe & Userflow

Installer Onboarding

To streamline the installer onboarding process and enhance the user experience, the web application will be designed to guide the installers through easy-to-follow steps, which will include a clear overview of the requirements, a simple form for inputting relevant information, an option to upload required documentation, a final review step to ensure accuracy, and a confirmation screen with a personalized message to the installer.

Installer Dashboard

The goal is to ensure that the dashboard is intuitive, visually clear, and efficient in terms of allowing the user to access the information they need.

Projects

Displays available projects with project details and allows installers to search for specific projects. Installers can export project details for their records.

Admin Dashboard

The admin dashboard provides quick access to the latest projects and bids, as well as an overview of upcoming appointments for easy management.

Customer

Allowing the admin to easily monitor project progress and customer satisfaction and Status of the projects

Installer

providing admin with a comprehensive view of the user details, project type, project status, and assignment status for efficient project management and tracking.

Outcome

Sunsource has resulted in a significant increase in user engagement and conversions. Users are now able to easily estimate their potential cost savings, financial preferences, and upload their utility graph for a more accurate assessment. As a result, Sunsource has seen a 40% increase in user sign-ups and a 25% increase in completed solar projects, leading to a significant increase in revenue for the company
