Sunsource

More sustainable, efficient, and cost-effective energy solution for your home.

Sunsource is the easiest way to find and install solar products for your home. With a wide range of solar panels, you can connect with reputable installer and get expert guidance from Sunsource's Energy Advisors. sunsource makes it easy to find the right products to meet your energy needs.

Problem

Finding reputable solar product installers and receiving expert guidance.

Homeowners who are interested in transitioning to solar energy face a number of challenges when trying to find reputable solar product manufacturers and installers, Energy advisor's and receive expert guidance and support throughout the buying and installation process.

Goal

Web app that simplifies the transition to solar energy for homeowners

The goal of this project is to design a user-friendly web app that addresses these challenges by providing homeowners with a comprehensive way, connecting them with reputable installers, and offering expert guidance and support throughout the buying and installation process. The web app will enable homeowners to easily compare and choose between different solar products based on transparent pricing and product information, and will provide a seamless user experience that simplifies the transition to solar energy.

Design Process

  • Discover: Identify Problem, Research, Competitive analysis, Analyze data
  • Define: Information architecture, Presona's, User journey
  • Ideate: Userflow, Lo-fi design, Wireframe
  • Prototype: Hi-Fi mockup, Interactive prototype
  • User Testing: Usability Testing, Task Tracking
  • Launch: Refined Designs, Release, Gathering new data

User research

We conducted user interviews and surveys to better understand the pain points and needs of homeowners interested in transitioning to solar energy, and to inform the design of a web app that addresses these challenges.

Findings:

  • Many homeowners are interested in transitioning to solar energy but struggle to find reputable solar product manufacturers and installers.
  • Homeowners are hesitant to invest in solar energy without expert guidance and support throughout the buying and installation process.
  • The user experience of existing solar marketplaces is often confusing and overwhelming, with limited guidance and support available.

Pain Points:

  • Difficulty finding reputable solar product manufacturers and installers.
  • Lack of expert guidance and support throughout the buying and installation process.
  • Confusing and overwhelming user experience of existing solar marketplaces.

Wireframe & Userflow

Solar Estimator

Solar Estimator tool calculates your average electric bill based on your location and shows you the cost savings of switching to solar power, so you can make an informed financial decision and start saving today.

Dashboard

users can easily schedule or reschedule appointments for solar installation at multiple addresses through a user-friendly interface. The feature can also keep track of each project's status.

Upload utility

User will be presented with a saving summary that shows available rebates, new vs old energy bill, and lifetime savings. Additionally, users can upload their utility graph using a file upload input and preview the uploaded file in a separate section for a more seamless and efficient experience.

Bid Review

Allows you to select financial preferences available for your solar project. You can compare each bid's and choose the one that best fits your needs. Once you have made your decision, you can add the chosen bid to your cart and along with the total contract price, you can proceed to checkout.

FAQ

Provides answers to frequently asked questions about solar energy, installation, costs, rebates, and more. You can browse through our comprehensive list of questions and answers.

Outcome

Sunsource has resulted in a significant increase in user engagement and conversions. Users are now able to easily estimate their potential cost savings, financial preferences, and upload their utility graph for a more accurate assessment. As a result, Sunsource has seen a 40% increase in user sign-ups and a 25% increase in completed solar projects, leading to a significant increase in revenue for the company
