Carcare

Effortlessly Manage Your Car's Maintenance with CarCare

Carcare is for car owners to easily track their vehicle's maintenance and repair needs. With features such as service record tracking, appointment reminders, and local service center lookup, you can keep your car running smoothly without any hassle. It also helps you track your car's fuel economy and mileage. Overall, Carcare is a convenient and powerful tool for any car owner.

Project Type

Client Work

Timeline

2021 - 2022

My Responsibilities

Platforms

IOS & Android

Discovery

Creating a user-friendly auto service app to streamline car maintenance and enhance accessibility

We discovered that many people find the car maintenance process overwhelming and struggle to find reliable service providers. They also expressed frustration with keeping track of their maintenance history and understanding what services are necessary for their vehicles. Based on this feedback, our solution is to create an app that simplifies the process of booking appointments, finding trustworthy service providers, and tracking maintenance history. The app will also provide personalized maintenance recommendations based on the specific needs of each user's car.

User Interview

Insights from the interviews

  • Users find car maintenance a hassle and want an app that simplifies the process.
  • Personalized recommendations for car maintenance are important to users.
  • Online appointment booking and automatic reminders for service needs are valued.
  • Users want a maintenance history tracker in the app.
  • Users prioritize the safety and performance of their vehicle and want an app that offers maintenance recommendations.
WHO

Personas

The lack of a comprehensive and user-friendly app for managing car maintenance and service is causing frustration and inefficiencies for users like Max and Sarah, leading to missed appointments, unnecessary expenses, and overall dissatisfaction with the car maintenance experience.

Journey map

After mapping user journey with the carcare app, it was found that the app would provide a seamless and convenient experience for scheduling, tracking and paying for services, which meets user's expectations and needs. However, the app could benefit from improving the transparency and accuracy of service estimates, as well as providing more personalized and proactive communication throughout the service process. These improvements have the potential to increase user's trust and loyalty towards the app and the auto service providers that use it.

competitor analysis

It can be concluded that there are common features such as booking appointments, online payment, and diagnostic services offered by all these apps. However, each app has its unique features such as on-demand services, car warranty protection, and loyalty programs, which makes them stand out from their competitors. Overall, it is essential for Carcare to offer a combination of these features to remain competitive in the auto service industry.

The Problem

There is a lack of a comprehensive and user-friendly auto service app that can provide a seamless experience for car owners in managing their car maintenance and repair need. Inconsistent service quality, and complex user interfaces, leading to frustration and inconvenience for car owners.

The solution

Mobile app that offers a user-friendly and convenient way for car owners to book and track their auto services. By integrating various features such as an appointment scheduler, service tracking, and payment system, Roadside assistance, Maintenance discounts. we aim to streamline the entire process of car maintenance and repair, providing a seamless experience for users. Additionally, our app will offer personalized recommendations based on the user's car model and maintenance history, ensuring that they receive the best possible service and care for their vehicle.

Home

Allowing users to have a prominent way to acess claims and road assistance, ensuring easy access to help when needed. Users can view their vehicle list and service history, with detailed service details and allows user to access to digital copies of insurance ID cards and vehicle contracts.

Service

Providing user with total service records for each vehicle. Users can add new service records and upload the history of previous service, ensuring that all the necessary information is in one place. With the Carcare service, users can easily track their vehicle's maintenance history and ensure that it stays in top condition.

Acivity

Providing users with a clear overview of their vehicle information, including current and previous mileage readings, as well as a convenient way to add new mileage. Additionally, users can easily track the history of their mileage updates and view the status of their claims with just a few clicks. With this comprehensive view of their vehicle activity, Wellcar users can stay on top of their vehicle maintenance and make informed decisions about future repairs and service needs.

Rewards

Providing user's with current reward point balance along with the history of points credited for using services on the app. Users can also redeem the points they wish to redeem. The page is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, with clear information about the available rewards and how to redeem them.
